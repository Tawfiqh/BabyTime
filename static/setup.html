📱 Device Setup

One-time setup to trust BabyTime's local certificate

⚠️ This device does not yet trust the BabyTime certificate.
Follow the steps below — it only takes a minute.
1
Download the certificate

Tap the button below to download the BabyTime local CA certificate.

⬇️ Download Certificate
After tapping, iOS will say "Profile Downloaded" in a notification at the top of the screen.
2
Install the profile

Open Settings → General → VPN & Device Management

Tap the mkcert profile, then tap Install. Enter your passcode if prompted.

3
Enable full trust (critical — easy to miss)

Open Settings → General → About → Certificate Trust Settings

Toggle ON the mkcert certificate under "Enable Full Trust For Root Certificates".

Without this step, iOS installs the certificate but still doesn't trust it for HTTPS. The camera will still be blocked.
4
Reload this page

Come back to Safari and reload this page. The warning banner above should turn green. ✓

→ Go to BabyTime
